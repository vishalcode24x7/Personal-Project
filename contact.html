Before You Contact Us

You might find answers to common questions here:

How do I use the font generator?

Simply type your text in the input box on our homepage, and multiple styled fonts will appear instantly. Click any style to copy it.

Are TextAura tools really free?

Yes! All our tools are 100% free with no subscriptions or hidden costs. Learn more on our About page.

Why isn't a font displaying correctly?

Most Unicode fonts work everywhere, but some older devices may have limited support. Try a different style or update your device.

Can I use TextAura for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! All text generated with TextAura can be used freely for personal and commercial projects.
