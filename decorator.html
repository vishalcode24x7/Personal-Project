Will decorated text work on Instagram?

Absolutely! All our decorated text styles use Unicode characters that are fully supported by Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook, TikTok, and all major social media platforms.

How do I copy decorated text?

Simply click on any decorated text style you like, and it will be automatically copied to your clipboard. Then paste it anywhere using Ctrl+V (Windows) or Cmd+V (Mac).

Why doesn't some text display correctly?

Most modern devices support all Unicode characters, but some older devices or apps may have limited support. If a style doesn't display correctly, try a different style or update your device.

What's the difference between text decorator and font generator?

A text decorator applies various styling to your text using Unicode characters, while a font generator typically offers different font families. Our homepage combines both for maximum options!

No! TextAura's text decorator works entirely in your web browser. No downloads, installations, or special software required. Just type and copy!
