What is a QR Code Generator?

A QR Code Generator is a free online tool that creates QR codes (Quick Response codes) from text, URLs, phone numbers, or any data. QR codes are scannable images that smartphones can read instantly using their camera, making them perfect for quick information sharing in the digital age.

TextAura's QR code maker is completely free, requires no registration, and generates high-quality QR codes that you can download as PNG images. Whether you need QR codes for business cards, marketing campaigns, product packaging, or event posters, our tool makes it easy!

  • Offline to Online: Bridge physical materials with digital content seamlessly

Tips for Creating Effective QR Codes

  • Use Short URLs: Shorten long URLs before converting to QR codes for better scanning
  • Test Before Printing: Always scan your QR code with multiple devices before mass printing
  • Ensure Contrast: Print on light backgrounds for maximum scanability
  • Size Matters: Use at least 200x200 pixels for printed materials
  • Add Context: Include text like "Scan for more info" near your QR code
  • Leave White Space: Don't crop too close to the QR code edges

QR Code Best Practices

To ensure your QR codes work flawlessly:

  1. Minimum Size: Print at least 2x2 cm (0.8x0.8 inches) for reliable scanning
  2. Error Correction: QR codes have built-in error correction, allowing them to work even if partially damaged
  3. Color Guidelines: Dark QR code on light background works best (black on white is ideal)
  4. Placement: Put QR codes in easily accessible locations at comfortable scanning heights
  5. Landing Pages: Ensure linked pages are mobile-optimized since most scans happen on phones

Frequently Asked Questions About QR Codes

Is this QR code generator really free?

Yes! Our QR code generator is 100% free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or limitations. Generate and download as many QR codes as you need without any registration.

What size QR code should I use?

For digital use (websites, social media), 200x200 pixels works well. For printing on business cards or flyers, use 300x300 pixels or larger to ensure scannability.

Do QR codes expire?

No! QR codes generated with our tool never expire. They're static codes that will work forever, as long as the content you encoded (like a URL) remains active.

Can I edit a QR code after creating it?

QR codes are static once generated. To change the content, you'll need to create a new QR code. However, if you use URL shorteners with redirect capabilities, you can change the destination while keeping the same QR code.

How do I scan a QR code?

Most modern smartphones can scan QR codes directly through their camera app. Simply open your camera, point it at the QR code, and tap the notification that appears. Alternatively, use dedicated QR scanner apps.

Why isn't my QR code scanning?

Common issues include: QR code is too small, poor lighting, QR code is damaged or blurry, insufficient contrast between code and background. Try generating a larger size or testing with different devices.

Yes! QR codes generated with TextAura are free to use for personal and commercial purposes. No attribution required. Use them on business cards, products, marketing materials, or anywhere you need.
